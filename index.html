// 03 SIGNAL TYPES

Three ways things go quiet before they go wrong.

Most outages announce themselves early — in latency, in error rate, in silence. Pulse is built to hear each one.

Heartbeat checks

Every endpoint, queue, and cron job pinged on the interval that matches its risk — seconds for payments, minutes for batch jobs.

Drift detection

Pulse learns each service's normal shape and flags the slow creep in latency or error rate that thresholds miss entirely.

Dependency mapping

See which downstream failure actually caused the incident, instead of triaging six false alarms from six symptoms of one cause.
